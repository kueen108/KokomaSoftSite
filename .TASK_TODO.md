# KokomaSoftSite Improvement Checklist

## Research Summary
- The current site is a multilingual company/app index with store links and policy pages, but it gives limited help for deciding which app to install.
- All listed apps have Android projects under `~/git`; launcher icons are already imported into `public/app-icons`.
- Store-ready screenshots are available for `MindfulEcho` and `UngyeolAos`, but not for `LifeLottoAos` or `MyTubeAos` in the local project folders found during this pass.
- The highest-value first step is to add structured app detail content, clear user benefits, platform/support links, and better navigation from the home page.
